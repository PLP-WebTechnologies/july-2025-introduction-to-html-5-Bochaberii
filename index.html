About Sustainable Living

Sustainable living means making choices that reduce our impact on the environment while maintaining a good quality of life.

Why It Matters

Small changes in our daily habits can help protect the planet for future generations.

Simple Eco Tips

Save Energy

  • Use LED light bulbs
  • Unplug devices when not in use
  • Use natural light during the day

Save Water

  1. Fix leaky faucets
  2. Take shorter showers
  3. Run full loads in dishwashers

Reduce Waste

Follow the 3 R's:

Reduce
Buy less stuff
Reuse
Find new uses for old items
Recycle
Sort waste properly

Quick Fact

Recycling one aluminum can saves enough energy to run a TV for 3 hours!
